So you’ve heard of this OpenAtrium thing, and heard the chatter about Context, Features and Spaces. You’ve installed Atrium, but the Black Box is confusing.

This session will:

  • Briefly recap the features and functionality of OpenAtrium as a Community & Intranet-targeted Drupal Distribution.
  • Explain the concepts behind Context, Spaces, and Features that make it possible.
  • Demonstrate how to customize your own OpenAtrium site.
  • Go over a Case Study for an OpenAtrium site built as a Drupal Community.

Co-Presenting with Jim Woods/redkilian.
