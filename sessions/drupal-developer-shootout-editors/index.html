Still getting familiar with a new text editor (or IDE) someone recommended for Drupal work? Now you feel either stuck with it or scared to try out another as you are too busy and have work to do! Fear not web heads, your friendly neighborhood Drupal developers are going to give you a Developer Shootout. Multiple speakers will each showcase their editors and demo the whats/wheres/whys its their choice for Drupal/PHP development.

May the best editor to you win.

Editors & IDEs planned for this shootout are:

  • Eclipse IDE – http://eclipse.org (free; demo by Chris Charlton)

  • Netbeans IDE – http://netbeans.org (free; demo by Rich Yumul)

  • Adobe Dreamweaver CS5 – http://adobe.com/dreamweaver (commercial; demo by Chris Charlton)

  • Komodo Edit – http://www.activestate.com/komodo-edit (free; demo by Ashok Modi)

Have an cross-platform editor/IDE you’d like to show how good it can code your Drupal (PHP) code? Then comment below to participate.
