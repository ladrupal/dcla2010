Humping Drupal

yeah, yeah what’s in a title?! so now get your mind outta the gutter and back into drupal… this is the beginners guide to getting over the drupal “hump.”

do you have 15 years experience building websites or is drupal your first crush? either way, if you’re still relatively new to drupal, you’ve been stuck, and you’ve wondered what kind of crazies built this thing. my goal in this session is to help you understand more of the drupal plumbing and to give you some strategies to get you over the hump from beginner to pro.

hope you get the idea, this will be a fun session for drupal newbies and at the very least you’ll leave the session with some new friends with whom you can commiserate. :-P
